Intern | Research Foundation of CUNY with Crown Doctors and Digital Factory Inc

My role as a Software Development Intern was an enriching and transformative experience that allowed me to contribute significantly to a cross-platform app project while developing leadership and project management skills.


Accomplishments:

Cross-Platform App Development: I was tasked with spearheading the development of a cross-platform mobile application. Leveraging my technical skills, I utilized React Native with Expo-CLI, Google Firebase, and the Google Places API to create a robust and user-friendly app. This experience enabled me to gain hands-on expertise in cutting-edge technologies used in modern app development.

Leadership and Team Management: As part of my role, I had the opportunity to lead a team of 10 dedicated app developers. I organized and facilitated weekly team meetings, fostering effective communication and collaboration among team members. Additionally, I presented regular progress reports to ensure that the project remained on track and met its scheduled milestones. This experience honed my leadership abilities, enhancing my capacity to guide and motivate a diverse group of professionals towards a common goal.

Social Impact: Beyond the technical aspects of the internship, I was deeply motivated by the project's noble objective. Our mission was to contribute to Crown Doctors' ongoing program to raise awareness about alopecia. This experience allowed me to connect my passion for technology with a meaningful cause, highlighting my commitment to projects that make a positive impact on society.

Key Takeaways:

My internship with the Research Foundation of CUNY, Crown Doctors, and Digital Factory Inc provided me with a comprehensive learning experience. It allowed me to acquire technical skills in mobile app development and leadership skills in project management. Moreover, the opportunity to work on a project with a significant social impact was personally fulfilling and reinforced my dedication to using technology to address real-world issues.
