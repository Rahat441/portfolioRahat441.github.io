New York City Department of Transportation

Software Development Intern
Jan 2022 - May 2022

Overview : During my tenure as a Software Development Intern at the Department of Transportation (DOT), I had the opportunity to work on a unique and impactful project. My role revolved around utilizing Unity with C# to develop an educational app that simulates the experience of driving a truck in a lifelike version of Manhattan, all while educating users about the obstacles and challenges associated with operating a large vehicle.

Accomplishments

Educational App Development: I spearheaded the development of this educational app, where realism was a top priority. To achieve this, I meticulously incorporated realistic physics, including gravity and resistance, to enhance the immersive experience. This involved a deep understanding of physics principles and programming expertise in Unity with C#. Project Scope and Objectives: I took charge of planning and delivering the project's scope and objectives. The ultimate goal was to create an interactive and engaging educational tool that would promote a culture of safety. This tool was seamlessly integrated into the DOT's Truck Eye View Program, contributing to the department's commitment to safety and awareness.

Key Highlights:

My internship at the Department of Transportation was an enriching experience that not only allowed me to refine my technical skills but also demonstrated the real-world impact of technology on safety and education. It reinforced my belief in the power of innovative software solutions to address critical issues and promote positive change. I am excited to continue applying my software development expertise to projects that have a meaningful impact on society. My experience at the DOT has deepened my commitment to creating interactive and educational tools that drive positive change, and I look forward to discussing how my skills and passion align with your organization's mission and objectives.
